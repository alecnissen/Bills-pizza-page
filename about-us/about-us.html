About us

Both Courtney and I had a passion for cooking, and for serving others. We decided we wanted a restraunt that serves as a home and a community of people in the neighborhood not just serving pizza. We made it a goal to make the best quality pizza, best quality local catering options, and the best sports bar in the area. We are proud to serve the community and to get involved in the community. Our specialty is our buffalo chicken pizza with wings and don't forget to try our signitaure drink, the bow and arrow! We have an excellent sports bar including wide screen TV's, dart's and a pool table!
